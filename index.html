About Us:

We are a small team of DangerZone players / modders who enjoy doing stupid stuff. We formed when "Cainho" DMed DeBeast saying that it would be cool if there was a DZ server, so DeBeast talked to TheyCallMeDanger about it. Later Danger said that he enjoys building computers, so DeBeast (As a joke) said "build us a computer for a server" and he actually agreed to it, then DeBeast and Cainho got a small team of people we wanted to join the server, we ended up with Cainho, Danger, Spooder Man / DiamondKidd, WeirdNerd / InvalidName, Xahann, and DeBeast591. Later, DeBeast asked if the team should be renamed from just "duck smp" to "the quackpack" and we agreed to it.
